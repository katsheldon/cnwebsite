What are dinosaurs?


  • Dinosaurs are a group of reptiles that were around for over 140 million years.
  • They were diverse shapes and sizes and lived in a variety of ecosystems.

Did you know?


  • Non-bird dinosaurs lived between 245 and 66 million years ago
  • This time is known as the Mesozoic Era and is split into the
    • Triassic Period (252 to 201 million years ago)
    • Jurassic Period (201 to 145 million years ago)
    • Cretaceous Period (145 to 66 million years ago)
  • During this time the land split from one huge continenet into smaller ones
  • The changes in climate and vegetation affected how dinosaurs evolved
